PKL Ball is a free app built for pickleball communities — manage ladder leagues, sign up for sessions, track your ranking, and compete with players at your level.
🏆
Ladder Rankings
Move up or down the ladder based on your results. See exactly where you stand among local players.
📋
Sign-Up Sheets
Register for upcoming sessions online. Get on the waitlist automatically if a spot opens up.
🎾
Court Assignments
Courts are automatically assigned by skill level so every match is competitive and fair.
📊
Score Tracking
Submit scores from your phone right on the court. Stats and rankings update in real time.
🔔
Push Notifications
Get notified when sign-ups open, when you move on the ladder, or when sessions start.
👥
Private Groups
Join invite-only groups for your regular crew, or discover public leagues open to all players.
